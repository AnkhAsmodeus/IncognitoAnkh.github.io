-Terms of Service-

Applied only to direct commissions.

If you request a commission, I will assume that you have read and agree to the following terms.

Workflow

Payment > First Version > Revisions > Final Delivery

• All my commissions are delivered “as soon as possible”.
• I take feedback throughout the project to have the product correctly at the final delivery.
• The "Revisions" step can be repeated until you are satisfied with the product at no additional cost, as long as the complexity and number of revisions is fair to both parties (no drastic changes such as redoing the product).
• Orders will be made in queue order and delivery priority.

Payments

Payments can be made through PayPal or Stripe either in advance or in halves:

• I will start working once at least the first half of the project price is paid.
• I will send you a preview each time i make substantial progress for feedback. If you paid full price up front, you can also request earlier versions of the product to try it out for yourself without waiting for final delivery.
• I will deliver the final product once the total price of the commission has been paid.
• During the 7 days following the final delivery you can request an extra review for small adjustments at no additional cost.

Dedicated Works
• Aimed at people or teams that need my services as close as possible, such as game development teams or large projects with many required assets, payment and workflow to be discussed.

License

By paying the price of a Commission you are acquiring a personal license for the use of the requested product that allows you to:

• Use the product for personal purposes.
• Distribute the product for free.
• Use the product for commercial purposes as long as it is integrated into another project which may include, but is not limited to, video games, print products, videos, live streams, advertisements, emotes, profile pictures, and social media banners.

You are not authorized:
• Claim to be the creator of the product, you must always properly credit IncognitoAnkh.
• Resell the product itself or with slight modifications, either individually or in asset packs.
• Create NFTs with the product.

I am always willing to discuss the above terms. I may use the product for purposes related to self-promotion which may include, but are not limited to, self-advertising, live streaming, video, and portfolio (if you require a temporary period of confidentiality, be sure to let me know).
