-Terms of Service-

Applied to Direct commissions only.

If you ask for a commission, I’m going to assume you read and agree to these terms.

Workflow

Payment > First Version > Revisions > Final Delivery

• All my commissions are delivered “ASAP”.
• I take feedback all along the way to get the product right at delivery.
• “Revisions” step can be repeated until you are satisfied with the product without additional cost if the complexity and number of revisions is fair for both parts (NO full asset redo).
• Orders will be made in order of queue and delivery priority.

Payments

Payments can be made through PayPal in any of the following ways:

In Advance or Halves
• I will start to work once at least the first halve of the project price is paid.
• I will send to you a preview every time I have a substantial progress for feedback, if you paid the full price in advance you can also request early versions of the product to test it yourself without having to wait for the final delivery.
• I will delivery the final product once the full price of the commission is paid.
• During the period of next 7 days after the final delivery you can request one extra revision for small adjustments without additional cost.

Dedicated Works
• Intended for people who needs my services in a closest way like game development teams or big projects with a lot of assets required, the payment and workflow need to be discussed.

Use Terms

Applied by default to all my works, i’m always open to discuss and negotiate this agreement.

• You can use the product for commercial purposes if is integrate into another project (game, printed merchandise, video, etc.) but not if you resell it on its own (Asset bundle, NFT, etc.).
• For non-personal use, regardless of whether it is commercial or not, you must properly credit IncognitoAnkh.
• You can’t claim yourself as the creator of the product.
• I can use the product for portfolio or self-promotion purposes (If a temporary period of confidentiality is needed be sure to tell me when ordering).
• I can showcase the process of make the product (e.g., video, or live stream) unless you request me to don’t do it.
